What is Crest?

Crest is a Smart Ad Campaign token. Crest DigiAd Platform runs on Crest Token.

Crest DigiAd is an online marketing and digital advertising platform for Advertisers and Publishers, designed to run crypto-to-crypto ads and non-crypto related campaigns on the blockchain.

The Crest ecosystem allow members of the Crest Community to generate income by allocating Crest utility tokens to run several marketing and advertising campaigns which in-turn generates income for the community.
