Terms & Conditions

Contest runs for exactly 10 days from 28th August to 7th September ending at 8am EST.

Contest Minimums are as follows:
1st: Earn 16,000 CSTT in Total Commissions
2nd: Earn 8000 CSTT in Total Commissions
3rd: Earn 6000 CSTT in Total Commissions
4th: Earn 4000 CSTT in Total Commissions
5th: Earn 2000 CSTT in Total Commissions
6th - 7th: Earn 1000 CSTT in Total Commissions
8th - 9th: Earn 800 CSTT in Total Commissions
10th: Earn 400 CSTT in Total Commissions

If Minimums aren't hit for your prize level, the prize according to the lowest qualifying minimum will be paid out. EG: if you come in 4th and only earn 2000 CSTT in total commissions, you will be paid according to the highest prize level that qualified at 2000 CSTT, in this contest you'd be paid 5th place prize money.

Prize money will be paid directly into your Bitcoin wallet on your Crest dashboard after the contest ends.
